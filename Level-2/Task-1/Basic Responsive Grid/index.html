Lion

Lions are large cats that live in groups called prides. They are known for their majestic manes and powerful roars. Lions are apex predators and play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of their ecosystems.

Tiger

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family and are renowned for their strength and agility. They are solitary animals and are found in various habitats across Asia. Tigers are endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.

Cheetah

Cheetahs are the fastest land animals, capable of reaching speeds up to 60-70 miles per hour. They have slender bodies and distinctive black spots on their fur. Cheetahs are found in Africa and are primarily solitary animals.
